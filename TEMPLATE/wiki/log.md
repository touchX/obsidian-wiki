# Wiki Log

操作日志记录 Wiki 的重要变更。

## 日志格式

```markdown
## [YYYY-MM-DD] ingest | Title
- Extracted: 3 concepts, 2 entities
- Updated: [[page-x]], [[page-y]]

## [YYYY-MM-DD] query | Question
- Answered: 综合了 [[x]], [[y]]
- Filed to: [[synthesis/topic]]

## [YYYY-MM-DD] lint
- Found: N issues
- Fixed: [[page-x]], [[page-y]]
```
